Directorships


THE BURRELL COMPANY 1989 - 2008
Director with Andrew Burrell. Initially focussing on inner city restoration and regeneration projects. The company developed a track record with a reputation for “punching above its weight”. Publications ... in particular see Neil Baxters’s “Redesigning Scotland’s Cities”. Awards included Saltire, Cockburn, Homes for Scotland, RIAS, RIBA and so on www.theburrellcompany.co.uk


BUREDI 1999 - 2008

A joint venture between Burrell and EDI (City of Edinburgh’s development arm) was created between John Forbes and Ian Wall, then CEO of EDI.
Projects undertaken similar to the Burrell Company, but often with greater social inclusion...Housing for Homeless, nursery schools, libraries, public spaces etc. www.buredi.co.uk


PREMIER BURRELL LTD 2003 - 2008

A joint venture with David Murray’s property group- PPG. Introduced PPG to residential projects (hitherto solely commercial developers). A variety of projects completed in Edinburgh, Glasgow and Aberdeen.
